Dakshana Foundation

I have been a student of DAKSHANA FOUNDATION. DAKSHANA FOUNDATION is basically a NGO that helps bright and impoverished students predominantly from rural India to prepare for IIT and medical entrance exams for free of cost. I was enrolled there (Dakshana Bangalore Centre) in Two year programme for my JEE preparation. Dakshana operates from Irvine, California and Pune in India. Those two year were the best part of my life as I have had some of the best days of my life. It's been almost two years I left Dakshana but I feel like yesterday only I came out of this heaven. For more info. visit https://dakshana.org/
