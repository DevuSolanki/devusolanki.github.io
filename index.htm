Dakshana Foundation

Dakshana Foundation - Infinite good
I have been a student of DAKSHANA FOUNDATION.
It is basically a NGO that helps bright and impoverished students predominantly from rural India to prepare for IIT and medical entrance exams for free of cost.
I was enrolled there (Dakshana Bangalore Centre) in Two year programme for my JEE preparation. Dakshana operates from Irvine, California and Pune in India.
Those two year were the best part of my life as I have had some of the best days of my life. It's been almost two years I left Dakshana but I feel like yesterday only I came out of this heaven.
For more info. visit https://dakshana.org/

JNV.

Navodaya - A Home away from home.
Jawahar Navodaya Vidhyalayas(JNV) are residential schools from 5th to 12th all over the country, where students do not just study, they play together ,eat together ,make friends for life and grow together. I studied in Navodaya from my 6th to 10th only as I got selected in Dakshana Foundation in 11th. But I was part of JNV Bangalore in my 11th and 12th.
For a Navodayan JNV isn't just a school but a lot more than that. We cry the day we enter in the campus full of strangers and surely we cry the day we leave after 12th(+2). And after that for me at least it is not a schools it is a feeling, a bundle of emotions,and a family with whom I lived for five years.
For more info. visit https://navodaya.gov.in

Vigyan Manthan Yatra

Vigyan Manthan yatra - Mission Exellence
How it feels when someone tells you that you are going on a 10 days trip to Ahemedabad for free of cost? I felt that when I was in 9th standard.
Every year Madhya Pradesh council of science of technology(MPCST) organise Vigyan Manthan yatra / MP Mission Exellence in which they select students from all over the state MP and take them to visit great technological organisation or Institution of a perticular city our country India.
I was selected in VMY 2014-15, we were taken to Ahemedabad. I explored Space Application center(SAC, ISRO's Branch), AMUl( Asias largest dairy), Vikaram A. Sarabai community scince center, science city of Ahemedabad and many more. Best part was Science City, it was just amazing and felt very unrealistic. I must say if you ever visit Ahemedabad, do pay a visit to science city, you won't regret it. Good thing was I had no phone/camera at that time so focus was on to gain as much as I can and explore, not on to click a pic :) .
